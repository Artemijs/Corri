Is a solution for everyone who is learning a language and wants to practice and develop their speaking skills. The app doesn't just stop there, at some point in life we are all required to do a public speach or a presentation, it is a most common of fears for all. "Corri" will also benefit people who want to improve their public speaking skills.

Corri is an app that helps learning a new language easier than ever before. The app is designed for mobile devices and focuses on the two-way communication with the user. The built in Artificial Intelligence makes it possible to analyze and correct the users grammar and pronounciation. Navigate easily with voice commands and discover other functionalities such as online dictionary, voice recording and synonym suggestions.
